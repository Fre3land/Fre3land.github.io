Welcome to Nick's Moving Service

About

Nick's Moving Service is a Medford based business aimed at providing a labor service for people who are moving. The company aims at providing help in moving in or moving out of homes or apartments based around Tufts University campus.

Our hours of operations are: 7:00 am - 6:00 pm Mon-Sat.

Employees

All employees of Nick's Moving Service are Tufts University students. All of them are bright minded individuals who have been great in past history with no previous complications. They are background-checked as well to make sure of no previous complications.

Quality

Nick's Moving Service has a quality assurance policy. If any equipment is broken by one our employees, we will offer full reimbursement for damages. This typically never happens, but in the odd case it does, reimbursement is guaranteed.
